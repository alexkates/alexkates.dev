# **Features 🔍**

[supajournal.app](https://supajournal.app) just launched for the [Supabase Launch Week X Hackathon](https://supabase.com/blog/supabase-hackathon-lwx) and already has several important features to help you build that journaling habit.

## AI Generated Writing Prompts 🤖

For many, the hardest part of journaling everyday is finding inspiration. With [supajournal.app](http://supajournal.app), you no longer have to worry about running out of ideas or struggling to come up with topics.

![An image of a new supajournal entry with an AI generated prompt.](/blog/introducing-supajournal/image-03.png align="center")

Our AI-generated writing prompts are specifically designed to ignite your creativity and offer a starting point for your journal entries. The AI is optimized to encourage deep, meaningful, and insightful writing, helping you establish a journaling habit.

## Statistics 📈

You can't improve what you don't measure. It's crucial to track progress over time when developing any new habit. That's why Supajournal includes six key statistics as part of the MVP:

1. Journal Entries
    
2. Streak
    
3. Most Popular Word
    
4. Word Count
    
5. Words per Journal Entry
    
6. Most Recent Entry
    

![Image of Supajournal's statistics](/blog/introducing-supajournal/image-04.png align="center")

## Block Editor ✍🏻

Supajournal offers a [Notion](https://www.notion.so/)\-like editor experience. Powered by [novel.sh](http://novel.sh) and [TipTap](https://tiptap.dev/), this editor feels like a next-generation writing experience. The editor is composed of blocks, each of which can be modified independently from the others. You can drag, delete, edit, and customize each of these blocks to fit the mood you are in.

![Image of Supajournal's editor](/blog/introducing-supajournal/image-05.png align="center")

# Technology 🤖

## [Supabase](https://supabase.com)

This project was submitted as part of the [Supabase Launchweek X hackathon](https://www.madewithsupabase.com/p/supajournal). Supabase powers the Postgres database, authentication, and row-level security that form the core of the Supajournal app.

## [Open AI](https://platform.openai.com/docs/introduction)

At the core of Supajournal is its integration with OpenAI. Powered by the new [gpt-3.5-turbo model](https://platform.openai.com/docs/models/gpt-3-5), Supajournal generates thought-provoking journal prompts to help you overcome writer's block and engage in self-reflection.

## [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/)

I absolutely love Next.js. I think it's the best web development framework I've ever seen. Supajournal is using [Next.js 14](https://nextjs.org/blog/next-14) and utilizes several new features including React Server Components, App Router, and Server Actions.

## [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/)

Not much to say here other than it's 2023 and I've been using TypeScript by default for about 2 years.

## [Shadcn/ui](https://ui.shadcn.com/)

This was my first project using shadcn, and I must admit I was impressed. I genuinely appreciate the paradigm of installing a component without using NPM. It feels as if I own the components and can customize them however I need. Additionally, the components themselves feel incredibly clean and pleasant to use.
